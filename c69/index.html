Places You Must Visit

marina bay sands

i visited marina bay sands in 2022

Indulge in the best experiences at Singapore’s most iconic hotel – home to the world’s largest Infinity Pool. Lounge in your luxurious room and soak in unparalleled views of the city, or explore the integrated resort’s attractions, restaurants and shops.

gardens by the bay

i visited gardens by the bay in 2020

Gardens by the Bay is Singapore's premier horticultural attraction, and a city icon, spanning 101 hectares in the centre of the city featuring over 1.5 million plants. Step into the Flower Dome, the largest glass greenhouse in the world, as listed in the 2015 Guinness World Records, and the Cloud Forest, which is home to the world's tallest indoor waterfall. Explore the three waterfront gardens, spectacular cacti in the Sun Garden, and more.

Review on my favourite Movies

  Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate the MCU story line

I watched it today and I loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. However it is best ot watch all of the movies in chronological order instead, starting with Iron Man 1. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.

  Journey 2: The Mysterious Island
Amazing second film to the Journey Series

I watched it yesterday and I thoroughly enjoyed it. You don't need any previous knowledge of the story, and can simply watch the movie on its own. This movie was extremeley fun to watch, as it was funny and creative. The story is that a boy and his new step-father go on an adventure to find the boy's long lost grandfather, and end up on a mysterious island where leephants are tiny and lizards are huge. I 100% reccomend you to watch it!
